About Legendary Snake

Legendary Snake is an enhanced version of the classic Snake game with multiple levels, themes, and exciting gameplay.

Navigate the snake to eat food and grow longer, but be careful not to collide with yourself or obstacles!

As you progress through levels, you'll encounter new challenges including villagers running away from your snake!

Controls:

  • Arrow keys or on-screen buttons to move
  • P or Pause button to pause the game
  • M or Menu button to return to menu
